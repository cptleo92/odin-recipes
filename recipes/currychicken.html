Curry Chicken - Japanese Style

I can eat this all day. I love the savory and spicy curry taste, the smooth blend of chicken and rice, and all the vegetables thrown in for good measure. Mmm mmm mmm!

I'll teach you how to make this amazing dish. It's so simple, yet so good!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 drumsticks or equilavent weight in chicken thighs
  • 3 medium potatoes
  • 3 medium onions
  • 3 medium carrots
  • 3 medium cups of rice
  • 3 medium squares of curry roux
